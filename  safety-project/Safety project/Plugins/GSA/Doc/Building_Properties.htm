Building Properties

ANSI/BOMA Properties

These properties are calculated or determinate by SMC

Building R/U Ratio

Calculation rule: Building Rentable Area / (Office Rentable Area - sum of all Building Common spaces' Polygon Area). If GSA Calculation method is used, Sum of all floors' Polygon Areas / sum of all floors' Office polygon Areas and Store Polygon Areas.

Office Rentable Area

Sum of all Office, Store, and Building Common spaces' Polygon Areas in the building. If GSA Calculation method is used, Floor Common areas are also added.
